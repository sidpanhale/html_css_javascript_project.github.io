Chicken

Chicken is prepared as food in a wide variety of ways, varying by region and culture. It was one of the basic ingredients in the so-called white dish, a stew usually consisting of chicken and fried onions cooked in milk and seasoned with spices and sugar.

Beef

Beef is the culinary name for meat from bovines, especially cattle. Beef is the third most widely consumed meat in the world. US, Brazil, and the PRC are the world's three largest consumers of beef.

Sushi

Sushi is traditionally made with medium-grain white rice, though it can be prepared with brown rice or short-grain rice. It is very often prepared with seafood, such as squid, eel, yellowtail, salmon, tuna or imitation crab meat.
